Game Instructions

Welcome to GeoGallery! Here's how to play:

  1. Enter a Room ID and your name, then click "Create Room"
  2. Share the game link and the Room ID with a friend
  3. Click on the map to place your guess of the location depicted in each painting
  4. After all players have guessed (or if you are playing solo) the real location is shown on the map

Good luck!
